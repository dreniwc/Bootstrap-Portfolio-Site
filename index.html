Who is Callum Dreniw?

I chose to study Information and Communications at Manchester Metropolitan University as this course stood out to me having studied ICT as an A level and completed a subsidiary BTEC in Media which I enjoyed. When choosing a university course that was right for me, I decided this course was best suited to what I wanted to study.

This course links to my long-term career aims - one of which is to be a web developer.

My interest and views for the world of web design have grown significantly. Keeping up-to-date with all the advancements and changes in the industry, I regularly research and read about web-related issues.
